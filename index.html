Jimmy's Favorite Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

As we all know, chocolate chip cookies cannot be beat. Follow this recipe for a perfect treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups - All purpose flour
  • 1 tsp - Salt
  • 1 tsp - Baking Soda
  • 3/4 cup - Brown Sugar firmly packed
  • 3/4 cup - Granulated Sugar
  • 1 cup - Softened butter or margarine
  • 1 tsp - Vanilla
  • 2 - Eggs
  • 1 pkg - Semi-Sweet or Mini Milk Chocolate Morsels

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Combine flour, salt and baking soda in bowl and set aside
  3. Combine brown sugar, granulated sugar, softened butter or margarine and vanilla and beat until creamy
  4. Add eggs and beat
  5. Add dry ingredients and mix well
  6. Stir in mini semi-sweet chocolate morsels
  7. Drop mixture by rounded teaspoonfuls onto non-greased cookie sheets
  8. Bake 8 to 10 minutes
